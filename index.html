Our Services

ACCOUNTING SERVICES

Our accounting services aimed at assisting our client to provide accurate, reliable and timely financial information that will assist the management in decision making.

INTERNAL AUDIT SERVICE

Our objectives in carrying out this function for our clients is to ensure that the company's assets are safeguarded, management principles and policies are adhered to, accounting records maintained are accurate and complete, management and financial accounts are reliable and are prepared on time and the company comply with all statutory obligations.

STATUTORY AUDIT SERVICES

Our statutory audit services satisfy the primary objective of expressing opinions on the fair presentation of the annual financial statements of organizations and ensuring compliance with the relevant regulatory provisions.

TRANSFER PRICING RETURNS SERVICES

The transfer pricing regulation require related or connected entities to prove that transactions between them are at arm's length. The objective of our transfer pricing returns services is to ensure accurate and timely filing of transfer pricing

CORPORATE TAX SERVICES

The main goal of our corporate tax services is to assist our clients to ensure that the tax liability in the audited accounts is correctly stated on a yearly basis and to facilitate the process of obtaining the companies tax clearance certificate.

PERSONAL INCOME TAX SERVICES

Using our personal income tax services, we will ensure that your employees are subjected to the minimum possible taxation in line with the provisions of the tax laws and with the aid of our tax efficient income distribution methods and obtain personal tax clearance certificates for each employee of the company on yearly basis.
